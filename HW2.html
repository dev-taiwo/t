The Best Movies According to Taiwo

My Top 3 Movies

Inception

I love this movie because it has an amazing storyline about dreams within dreams. The action scenes and plot twists keep me thinking the entire time.

Black Panther

This movie is one of my favorites because it combines action, culture, and powerful themes. The visuals and characters make it inspiring and exciting to watch.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

I enjoy this movie because it brings together different Spider-Man characters and has emotional and funny moments that make it unforgettable.
